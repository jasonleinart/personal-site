Most AI advice focuses on what to use. This session focuses on how to think.

A randomized controlled trial found that professionals who treated AI as a "teammate" achieved 85% accuracy on complex tasks, versus 75% without AI. Same technology. Different workflow. Better outcomes. A second study found six distinct interaction patterns — three preserved learning, three destroyed it. The split maps to three modes: Oracle, Offload, and Cognitive Collaboration.

This talk introduces the framework through live demos of a real marketing practice that depends on AI daily, then translates the architecture to five PM workflows: risk assessment, status prep, stakeholder communication, change analysis, and retrospectives. Attendees leave with a starter kit they can use Monday morning.
